My Personality Traits & Academic Life

My strongest traits are creativity, determination, and curiousity. I often express myself through art & design, which makes learning more visual and engaging for me. When I work on assignments, I like to make them stand out with layout, color, and presentation. My hobby of digital art and graphic design inspires me to think differently and helps me approach school work with a better mindset.

Personality Test Results

I completed the Similar Minds Personality Test, specifically the Attachment Style Personality Test. My results show that I lean toward an Avoidant Attachment (40%) with moderate levels of Disorganised Attachment (31%), while Secure and Anxious attachment styles are lower.

I think the test has some validity because it highlighted patterns I've noticed in myself. I often enjoy independence and can sometimes find it hard to open up right away, which fits the avoidant tendencies. However, as I've grown more confident and self aware, I've become stronger in communicating and balancing my emotions.
